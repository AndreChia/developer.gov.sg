The Engineering Suite Overview

Defining the Engineering Suite

Singapore Government Tech Stack (SGTS) offers a set of reusable components to streamline application development for agile system delivery. If there was a one-stop shop for you to discover, sign up and manage all the SGTS service, won't it be convenient? Introducing the Engineering Suite - the service management tool that will help agencies to subscribe and manage SGTS services from a single console.

A Singapore Government Tech Stack (SGTS) Product

The Engineering Suite belongs to a suite of Service Management components within the Base Layer of the SGTS.

Key features*

Engineering Suite aims to unify all SGTS services to make it easy for agencies to adopt the stack. We will be rolling out tools within Engineering Suite for:

  • Single Sign-On, Identity & Access Management solution for developer services in SGTS. This will enable users to access services and seamlessly transition between different SGTS services.

    TechPass Beta Coming Soon

  • Monitors & consolidates the usage metrics of SGTS services. The data is presented based on the profile of different stakeholders.

    Product Planning in Progress

  • Simplifies contract management & onboarding. Speeding up the onboarding process by removing repetitive steps and reusing the information we already have.

    Product Planning in Progress

  • Support service. A feedback & help centre that oversees and facilitates the “front-of-house” support for SGTS services.

    Product Planning in Progress

The first product in the pipeline is TechPass that allows users to use ONE set of credentials for all SGTS services.

Last updated 9 November 2020.

* Key features are subjected to changes based on user needs and strategic direction.
