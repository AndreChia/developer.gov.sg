Overview

Go.gov.sg is the official Singapore government link shortener, built by the Open Government Products team in GovTech.

An official link shortener allows government employees to:

  • craft URLs that are too long to fit into tweets or SMSes, and difficult to remember
  • send links to email clients that block other commercial link shorteners
  • send links to citizens who are afraid of phishing when receiving a shortened link and unsure of where it goes

With Go.gov.sg, citizens are safe in the knowledge that the links are official. Any public officer can log in with their government email and immediately create short links with the official gov.sg domain.

Get Started

Any public officer with an email ending in .gov.sg can use go.gov.sg with their gov.sg emails. They will receive a One Time Password that is sent to that email.

If you are a school staff member with an email ending in .edu.sg, please approach your school's IT team to ask about procuring a schools.gov.sg email address.
