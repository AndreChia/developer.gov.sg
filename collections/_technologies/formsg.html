Overview

FormSG is a form builder tool for government employees to create online forms capable of handling sensitive data, with the goal of replacing paper forms. Since it began in September 2017, it has replaced thousands of paper forms and is used by over 80 agencies and more than 10,000 public officers. It can be used be any public officers with gov.sg emails, with no on-boarding required.

How does it work?

FormSG has a simple to use interface to build your form. After you create your form, you may share your form link to your users. When users make form submissions, responses either go directly to your secure email inbox or are encrypted and stored on FormSG's servers, with the encryption key held by the form creator. For more information on storage modes, see the form.sg documentation.

What is the price?

FormSG is free to use.

Users that want to create forms with SingPass (SP) or CorpPass (CP) authentication will however have to pay for these external services. For more information on SPCP, see the form.sg documentation.

I am not part of the government. Can I use FormSG?

Government agencies and select government related organisations, such as public hospitals can use FormSG. If you are part of a private sector organisation that is keen on using FormSG, we have plans to release an open-source version in 2020. If you are part of a government related organisation, you may write in to formsg@tech.gov.sg to request for your organisation’s email domain to be whitelisted.

Get Started

  • Sign up for an account
  • Follow instructions to whitelist form.gov.sg emails so our emails don’t end up in your Junk folder.
  • Browse the Examples tab to explore forms created by other officers.
  • Start building your own form and publish it!
