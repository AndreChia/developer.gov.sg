Off-the-shelf web analytics tools require manpower to maintain and can be expensive. Here are some of the tools that GovTech has developed to help you better understand your users and make data-driven decisions.

WOGAA

WOGAA is an analytics tool that provides a customised dashboard which depicts the health status of your websites and digital services.
