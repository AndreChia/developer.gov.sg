Overview

The MCCY Grants Portal (MGP) brings government grants for arts, community, heritage, sports and youth into one place.

Now, it's easier to find and apply for grants you need to pursue your dreams - whether it is creating art, preserving history, winning a gold medal, or giving back to the community.

Benefits

Apply Easily
  • Simpler forms
  • Upload your documents
  • Save drafts and come back later

Get Updates on Your Application
  • Log in anytime to check on your application
  • Receive emails when the status of your application changes
  • Find out how long it will take for your application to be processed

Be Clear about What to do Next
  • See the drafts, applications and grants that need your attention on the dashboard
  • Every status update has a clear action
  • Consistent sections for all application forms
