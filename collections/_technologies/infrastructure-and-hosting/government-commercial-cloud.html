--- title: Government Commercial Cloud layout: layout-page-sidenav category: Hosting description: Designed to manage all the resources hosted on the commercial service providers, in the likes of Amazon Web Services (AWS), Microsoft Azure and Google Cloud Platform ---

The Singapore Government's less sensitive Government ICT systems can be hosted on commercial cloud providers, enabling the use of leading-edge private sector capabilities to develop digital services. A small number of very sensitive and critical systems will remain hosted within Government, even as the global norm is increasingly for ICT systems to be cloud-based.

Government Commercial Cloud (GCC) is a key part of the CODEX (Core Operations, Development Environment, and eXchange) which is the digital platform to enable the Government to deliver better digital services to citizens faster and more cost efficiently.

To find out more about the GCC, contact ask_gig@tech.gov.sg.
