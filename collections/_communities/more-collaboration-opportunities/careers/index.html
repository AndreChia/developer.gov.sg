Join the GovTech Family

Digital transformation within the public sector is at the heart of what the Government Technology Agency, or GovTech, does. We harness the best info-communications technologies to make a difference to the everyday lives of people in Singapore.

Our everyday work is to help build up our Smart Nation capabilities and expertise, fulfilling our mission of “Engineering Digital Government, Making Lives Better”.

At GovTech, we think big, start small and learn from failing fast. Our Agile, Bold and Collaborative values unite us from different teams in delivering business outcomes in a growth-orientated workplace. They help to drive what we build for our citizens, businesses and public officers.


Interested in joining the team that uses #techforpublicgood in our everyday work?

  • Learn more about what we do at www.tech.gov.sg

  • Apply with us at https://go.gov.sg/govtechcareers
