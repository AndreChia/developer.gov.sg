Overview

GovTech recently launched a five-week recruitment drive – a virtual “Tech Hunt” – taking place from 21 May to 21 June 2020.

We are looking to hire software engineers, digital business analysts, cybersecurity specialists, data scientists, AI engineers and infrastructure specialists, to help us design, build, and deliver tech solutions that will positively impact our citizens and businesses.

We will be hosting a series of webinars to let you in on what it is like working at GovTech. Upcoming webinars include:

  • 4 June: Securing our nation as cyber defenders

  • 11 June: Data for the public good

  • 12 June: Digital business analysts: applying our skills in #techforpublicgood

  • 18 June: Improving service agility with cloud

Interested applicants who believe in using tech for the public good can visit go.gov.sg/techhunt for more details.
