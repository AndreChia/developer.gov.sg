Edit an existing page

Requirements

To edit content on the Developer Portal, you simply need to:

  • be working in the Singapore Government
  • have instant access to your Government Email inbox for a one-time verification code
Step 1 - Edit content directly

Scroll to the bottom of your page and click on the "Edit This Page" button. You can then start editing your content in the pop-up text editor window.


Step 2 - Verify Government email

Once you are done editing, enter your Government (gov.sg) email and click on "Send OTP". Enter the verification code sent to your email, then click on "Submit Changes".

Your proposed changes can then be tracked via GitHub pull requests on the developer.gov.sg GitHub repo. Your submitted changes will be reviewed and approved or rejected within the next 5 working days. After approval, changes will be reflected almost instantly on the developer.gov.sg.
