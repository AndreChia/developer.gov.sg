--- title: Understand the broader environment layout: layout-page-sidenav ---

Not understanding regulations and expectations that come with any project can result in delays or even heavy costs associated with necessary changes discovered after various decisions have been made.

Examples of factors to consider while carrying out a project:

  • Conventions for products of a similar nature
  • Legal requirements
  • Policy requirements

It is the responsibility of the product owners and development teams to understand the policies and rules that affect the services they are building.

Understand technical decisions

Developers and architect must understand technical decisions made and the reasons behind them, and guide decisions using principles instead of what feels easy. Instead of committing to full-scale engineering straight away, it is also important to understand the tools and software already available, whether they be free, commercially available or already built by another team within the organisation.
