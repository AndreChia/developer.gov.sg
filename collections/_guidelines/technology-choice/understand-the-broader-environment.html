Factors to consider during a project

  • Conventions for products of a similar nature
  • Legal requirements
  • Policy requirements

Not understanding regulations and expectations that come with any project can result in delays or even heavy costs associated with necessary changes discovered after various decisions have been made.

It is the responsibility of the product owners and development teams to understand the policies and rules that they have to follow

Understand technical decisions and the reasons behind them. Guide decisions using principles instead of what feels easy. Instead of committing to heavy engineering straight away, it is important to understand the tools available, whether they be free, commercially available or already built by another team within the organisation.
