Benefits of open-source

Due to its numerous benefits, the usage of open source resources should be considered where possible:

  • Open source enables the construction and revamping of features using existing codes and products. This paves the way for both new innovations and the re-contribution of knowledge and resources into the industry.

  • Open source results in higher resource familiarity and prevalence of use, which leads to easier adoption, enhanced rates of adoption and easier hiring. 

  • Abundant support is available, as open source-related help is mostly freely available and can be easily accessed through online communities.

  • Open-source software can minimize cost expenses, as licensing and maintenance fees can be saved.
