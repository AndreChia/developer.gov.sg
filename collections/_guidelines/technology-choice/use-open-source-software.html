Overview

Where possible, teams should use open-source software. Benefits include:

  • Leverage on existing, well test code and software

  • Potential savings in licensing and maintenance fees

  • Higher resource familiarity and prevalence of use, which leads to easier adoption, enhanced rates of adoption and easier hiring

  • Abundant support is available, as open source-related help is mostly freely available and can be easily accessed through online communities

  • Lower barriers for new innovations and the re-contribution of knowledge and resources into the industry

Teams should note however that there are also costs associated with the usage of OSS, primarily in terms of manpower and migration costs.
