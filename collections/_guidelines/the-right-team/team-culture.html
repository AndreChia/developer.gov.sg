Effective teams

Effective teams stay cohesive over time and experience high satisfaction with with the meaning and impact their work. As a result, such teams often do not need strict rules restricting their actions. They often take the initiative to innovate and find ways to make meaningful contributions.

Discipline

Disciplined teammates know they can rely on each other to take charge of their work.

Culture of Excellence

Set the bar high in the beginning and keeping it there for the duration of your project:

  • Do not accept untested, poorly implemented features
  • Do not accept people abusing the build
  • Do not accept bugs

Ownership of product

Allow teams to have autonomy and ownership over their products. Ensure that every team member understands the objectives and goals of their product and what is expected of them.
