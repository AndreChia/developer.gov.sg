Overview

The following REST web-based application programming interface (API) standards guidance will help your agency deliver the best possible digital services to users. The aim is to encourage consistency, maintainability, and good practices within WOG, and balanced it with a positive developer's experience.

Demo and Beta Phase

This REST API guide is meant for showcasing (as of now) and the contents will continue to evolve.

Disclaimer

These guidelines only provide one standardized way of designing REST web-based APIs for most scenarios in the Government. Depending on your use cases, there could be other principles more suited for you that might not be covered in this guide.
