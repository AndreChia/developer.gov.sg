Overview

Here's how you can help the Singapore Government in its digital efforts to combat the COVID-19 pandemic.

Have a solution?

Here are 2 problem statements we are looking for input on:

  1. The Government is finding ways to quantify the benefits and costs to lessening circuit breaker measures and opening the economy. We would like to hear volunteer suggestions on models or optimisations (with parameters) that could guide policymakers in making decisions.

  2. How might we improve the contact tracing process to identify patterns in linked / unlinked cases so that we may generate the best qurantine yield possible in the shortest time and with the smallest manpower required through the use of technology and data? For the purpose of this problem statement, a close contact is defined as a person who has been in close proximity with an infected case within 2 metres and sustained for 30 minutes or more.

Solutions and ideas can be submitted here.

Be a volunteer

The Government has progressively built up the digital infrastructure and engineering capabilities as the foundation of our Smart Nation. This has enabled us to respond decisively and swiftly to the COVID-19 outbreak with a suite of digital tools to help disseminate timely and accurate information to Singaporeans and to enable our public agencies to better manage the crisis.

Refer below for more details about some products we’ve developed thus far, and the volunteering opportunities in which you can lend a helping hand.
