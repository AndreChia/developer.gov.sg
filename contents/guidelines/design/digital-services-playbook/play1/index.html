User Experience Process

UX is not something new. It's a shift in mindset that places people at the centre of the design process.

1.1 - Understand How Things Work Now

A key to transforming digital services is to understand the business inside-out.

1.2 - Understand the Landscape

A large part of innovation is about learning from predecessors.

1.3 - Understand the Users

User researches focuses on human behaviour, needs and motivations.

1.4 - Going Wide

Use cheap and fast methods to intentionally leave out the details to focus on the bigger picture.

1.5 - Deep-Dive

Move into higher-fidelity prototyping and continue testing regularly.

1.6 - Quantitative Testing

Quantitative gives context where the collected feedback allows you to understand your site's usability in the context of a much bigger picture.

1.7 - Qualitative Testing

Qualitative tells stories where the collected data can tell you which pages people visited, and how long they stayed and etc.
