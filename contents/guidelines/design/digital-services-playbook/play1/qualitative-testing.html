Qualitative tells stories where the collected data can tell you which pages people visited, and how long they stayed and where they came from and where they went to.

Qualitative Testing

You can try following these steps:

  • Recruit at least 5 subjects for each version of your prototype.
  • Introduce yourself and explain you are looking for problems in a new product, that if they have trouble, it’s the product’s fault, not theirs, that they shouldn’t feel bad.
  • Let them know it’s OK to quit at any time.
  • Go over the equipment - computers, camera, etc
  • Explain how to think aloud... and let them know you will not provide any help.
  • Describe the tasks and introduce the product.
  • Ask if there are any questions, then begin the test.
  • Conclude the test by thanking your subjects and asking for questions.
  • Apply the results and test again.
