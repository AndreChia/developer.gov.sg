Consider the landscape that the business is positioned in. What are other businesses doing? What is the technology available? Refine your business’ value propositions in light of all these considerations.

Competitive Audit

A large part of innovation is about learning from predecessors. Observe how other organisations have tried to solve similar problems, and where they have succeeded or failed. We don’t want to repeat the same mistakes that other people have made.
