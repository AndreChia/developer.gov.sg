Getting Started

These are the essentials for initiating any analytics project:

Clear Problem Statement & Actionable Impact

An idea of how, after if the analytics is done, your agency can do things differently. A clear quantifiable benefit from the analytics in mind.

Available & Relevant Data

About the factors contributing to the problem (demographics, profiles, institutional data) and the KPIs that need to be measured.

Buy-in From Key Stakeholders

A senior management sponsor must be responsible for both the policy or operational outcome, and the authorization for the release of data by IT departments.

This guide is designed as a simple primer for your adventure into analytics. We’ve written it as a set of beginner-friendly tools along the end-to-end life cycle of a data analytics project, to help teams set up and run their own analytics projects.

There is no need to have deep statistical knowledge, but a brief appreciation of statistical concepts will help you understand how to optimise for results.
