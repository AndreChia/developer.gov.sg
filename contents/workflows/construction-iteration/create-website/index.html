Overview

Whether you are building a static information, dynamic information or transactional website, we have tools to support your development.

Get Started

A static information website is the most basic kind of website, and the easiest to create. It requires no server-side processing, only client-side. Client-side technologies are HTML, CSS and JavaScript. In simpler terms, it requires no use of the back-end.

A static website is delivered to a user exactly the way it's stored. That means that nothing on the page will change by the user or even the site administrator, unless there's a redesign of the site or the site administrator goes directly into the code to change it.

Nothing is stored but the actual pages of the site. There are no users, no comments, no blog posts or any interactivity. No programming languages are required to make a static site.

All public facing digital services of the Government must meet the Digital Service Standards (DSS).

The Government conducts mystery-shopping checks based on the DSS, to ensure that our digital services are implemented consistently that are easy, seamless and relevant for our citizens and businesses.

GovTech provides Isomer to support government agencies in building static information websites while complying to DSS easily.

Isomer is an informational website generator powered by Markdown using GitHub. Isomer is free of charge and will cover hosting, maintenance, support and training for the system.

Email us at admin@isomer.gov.sg with your name, agency, and information about the website you want to build or migrate.

Customisation

Page Templates on Isomer are constructed with UI Components from the Singapore Government Design System, developed and maintain by GovTech.

Design System is a collection of reusable component, guided by clear standards, that can be assembled together to build any number of applications that comply to DSS.

If you wish to propose a new Page Template or UI Component that suits your needs better, please contact us here.
