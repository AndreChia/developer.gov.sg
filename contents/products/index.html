Products and Services

In this section, you will see the collections of products and services that the Singapore Government has to support your software development. By using these products and services, you are complying to the usage as well as security policies.

Analytics

WOGAA

Provides a set of common metrics and measurements to enable public agencies to conduct real-time monitoring.

Sentimeter

Tool to measure user satisfaction when interacting with government websites.

Blockchain

OpenCerts

OpenCerts lets you verify the certificates you have of anyone from any institution.

Business Application

FormSG

Sign up with your government email, and build your form in minutes. It's free and no approval is required.

OpenDoc

A simple and easy way to make documents easily searchable and available to the public.

GoSG

The official link shortener for the Singapore government.

Chat Bot

Ask Jamie

Virtual assistant (VA) that can be implemented on agency websites and trained to be able to answer queries within specific domains.

Content Management

Isomer

Provides template for website with hosting and is compliant to applicable clauses in the Instruction Manual 8 and Digital Service Standards.

A common secured environment based on a robust and controlled platform which provides a suite of standardised software for hosting content-based websites.

CI/CD/CT

SHIP

Provides managed services to enable teams to build applications rapidly without having to worry about hardware costs and operations overheads of maintaining these services.

HATS

A functional test automation tool for web and mobile applications.
