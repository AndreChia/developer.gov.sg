Requirements

Before you move on, here are items that you need in order to contribute to Developer Portal:

  • You must be working in the Singapore Government
  • You need to have instant access your Government Email for verification code
Step 1 - Edit Content

Scroll to the bottom of your page and click on "Edit This Page" button, then start editing your content in the pop-up window.

Step 2 - Verify Email

Once you are done editing, click on "Verify Government Email".

Enter the verification code sent to the address provided, then click on "Submit Changes for Review".

Your submission will be reviewed by a Content Moderator within the next 5 working days.
