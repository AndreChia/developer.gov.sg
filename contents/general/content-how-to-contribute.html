Get Started

Before you move on, here are items that you need in order to contribute to Developer Portal:

  • You must be working in the Singapore Government
  • You need to have instant access your Government Email for verification code
Tutorials
  • As a Contributor
  • As a Reviewer
Step 1 - Go to Page

Go to the page where you like to edit the content and click on the "Edit" button at the bottom-left of the page.

Step 2 - Edit the Content

A WYSIWYG editor will pop up where you can edit your content within it. Click on the "Send for Review" button when you are done.

Step 3 - Verify your Email

A verification code will be sent to your Government Email. Enter the code on the page and click "Submit".

Reviewer will be notified and update the page within the next 7 working days.

Step 1 - Go to Page

You will be notified for changes made to your page via your Government Email. Click on the link provided.

Step 2 - Review the Content

Click on the "Review" button and go through the differences between the new and current content.

Step 3 - Approve the Content

If everything looks fine, click on the "Approve" button.

Contributor of the page will be notified and page will be updated.
